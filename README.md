# ⚡ Electric Vehicle Business Report

---
## 📌 Project Overview

The **Electric Vehicle Business Report** is an interactive Business Intelligence project developed using **Microsoft Excel** and **Power BI**. The report analyzes India's Electric Vehicle (EV) market by exploring sales performance, market penetration, manufacturer performance, seasonal demand, and state-wise EV adoption.

This project transforms raw EV sales data into meaningful business insights through interactive dashboards, enabling stakeholders to monitor market trends, evaluate market performance, and support data-driven decision-making.

---

# 🎯 Business Objectives

- Analyze overall Electric Vehicle (EV) sales across India.
- Measure EV penetration rates across different states.
- Compare the market share of 2-Wheelers and 4-Wheelers.
- Identify the top and bottom-performing states based on EV adoption.
- Analyze manufacturer performance within the 4-Wheeler segment.
- Track monthly, quarterly, and yearly sales trends.
- Evaluate EV market growth using CAGR (Compound Annual Growth Rate).
- Compare the running costs of electric vehicles with conventional fuel vehicles.
- Generate business insights to support strategic planning and policy decisions.

# 💡 Key Insights

- Maharashtra recorded the highest Electric Vehicle sales among all states.
- Delhi achieved one of the highest EV penetration rates, indicating strong adoption relative to total vehicle sales.
- The dashboard highlights **Tata Motors, Mahindra & Mahindra, and MG Motor** as the leading manufacturers within the **4-Wheeler EV segment**.
- **2-Wheelers account for approximately 92.6% of total EV sales**, demonstrating their dominant contribution to India's EV market.
- January, February, March, November, and December recorded the highest EV sales, indicating seasonal demand patterns.
- Electric Vehicles have significantly lower running costs than petrol and diesel vehicles, making them a more economical long-term transportation option.
- Government incentives and the expansion of charging infrastructure continue to support the growth of EV adoption across India.

---

# 📌 Business Recommendations

- Expand charging infrastructure in high-growth and emerging EV markets.
- Continue government subsidy programs to encourage wider EV adoption.
- Increase awareness campaigns highlighting the long-term cost benefits of electric vehicles.
- Focus manufacturer expansion strategies in states with lower EV penetration.
- Plan inventory and marketing activities around peak seasonal demand periods.
